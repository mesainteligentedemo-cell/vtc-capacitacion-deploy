CRITICAL POINTS TO REMEMBER WHEN DOING ROLE-PLAY:

1. ALWAYS mention "TODAY" with emphasis - this is the psychological commitment
2. Always ask "Is that fair enough?" after the pact - wait for confirmation
3. Use their names (John and Mary) throughout to create rapport
4. When breaking down money, ALWAYS use worksheets or visuals
5. Never accept "I need to think about it" without asking follow-up questions
6. The 90 minutes reminder is CRITICAL - creates urgency
7. First visit incentives are ONLY valid today - repeat this
8. Hand shake and eye contact on the pact moment - this is the turning point
9. The three-way pitch MUST show difference between current way vs VTC way
10. Always end with a YES or NO question - never vague commitments
